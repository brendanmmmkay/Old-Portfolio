Bosch eBike Courier Connect

A smart phone application that leverages the Bosch eBike Systems technology to help couriers win time.

Context

Fall 2020 | 6 week Academic Experience Design Project in Art Direction, Strategy, Product Design, and Interaction Design

For a senior level design course, I was tasked with an ill-defined, client based project. Our mission was to find a company that could benefit from an experience design intervention.


Business Opportunity

Bosch is focusing on developing a stress-free experience for eBike riders, providing fitness tracking and wayfinding for recreational users. However, despite partnering with manufacturers that target eBike couriers, Bosch does not yet address their key problems with its current line of smart technology & software.

Design Opportunity

Help ebike couriers win time by aiding them to memorize their city and ride to the best of their abilities - in order to maximize their earnings.

Visual Direction

For the final presentation, I created a visual narrative to tell the context and pain points of the couriers. The goal was to imbue the rhythm and intensity of the job in images that told the story of our persona.
